Who I Am

About Me

I am a finance professional with CMA (US) exams cleared, currently working in Supply Chain Finance at Colgate Global Business Services, supporting manufacturing, commercial, copack, and contract manufacturing operations across North America and the Hill’s Pet Nutrition division.

My work sits at the intersection of operational data and financial decision-making, covering inventory costing, variance analysis, month-end close, and automation through VBA and SAP. I believe great finance teams don’t just report numbers, they explain what those numbers mean for the business.

I hold a Bachelor of Accountancy and Finance with an 8.7 CGPA and have been recognized with two scholarships. Beyond my role, I am passionate about building a stronger finance community in India through mentoring, campus sessions, and content on LinkedIn, where I share insights with 4,000+ professionals.

I am actively seeking FP&A, costing, and finance roles where I can contribute both analytical depth and a collaborative, execution-first mindset.
